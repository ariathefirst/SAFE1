The Stop Bullying for Everyone Campaign is organized to present accurate, balanced, and objective information about the prevalence and impact bullying and cyberattack bullying has children, adults, and communities. Our campaign consist of speakers who are approved by the SAFE International Board and represent a broad range of expertise. It is designed to be implemented into local schools and organizations as an Anti-Bullying Spirit Week or educational presentation.
